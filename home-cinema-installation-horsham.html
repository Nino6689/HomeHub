Frequently Asked Questions

How much does home cinema installation cost in Horsham?

TV wall mounting with cable concealment costs from £150-300. A soundbar setup with TV mounting runs £300-500. Full surround sound systems range from £800-2,000+. Dedicated cinema rooms with projectors start from £3,000. We offer free consultations to design within your budget.

Can you mount a TV on any wall?

Most walls can support a TV with the right fixings. We assess your wall type (plasterboard, brick, concrete) and use appropriate brackets and fixings rated for your TV's weight. We always ensure secure, safe mounting.

Do you hide all the cables?

Yes, we specialise in clean installations with hidden cables. For solid walls, we chase cables into the wall. For stud walls, we run cables inside the cavity. We can also use decorative solutions where concealment isn't possible.

What's better - soundbar or surround sound?

Soundbars are great for improving TV audio without multiple speakers - ideal for smaller rooms. True surround sound provides immersive audio from all directions - best for dedicated cinema rooms or larger living spaces.
