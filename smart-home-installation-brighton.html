Frequently Asked Questions

How much does smart home installation cost in Brighton?

Smart home installation in Brighton varies based on scope. A starter package with voice control, smart lighting for 2-3 rooms, and a smart thermostat costs from £600-900. Whole-home automation with lighting, heating, security, and multi-room audio ranges from £2,000-5,000+. We offer free consultations to design a system that fits your budget.

Do you work with period properties in Brighton?

Yes, we have extensive experience with Brighton's Regency and Victorian properties. We use wireless smart home technology that requires no rewiring, preserving original features. Smart switches fit existing switch plates, and wireless sensors install without drilling into decorative plasterwork.

Which smart home platform is best?

Each has strengths: Alexa offers the widest device compatibility, Google excels at natural conversation and search, Apple HomeKit provides the best privacy and works seamlessly with iPhones. We can help you choose based on your existing devices and priorities, or create systems that work across all platforms.

Can smart home technology reduce my energy bills?

Absolutely. Smart thermostats alone typically save 10-15% on heating bills by learning your schedule and optimising temperatures. Smart lighting with motion sensors eliminates wasted electricity. Many Brighton customers see their systems pay for themselves within 2-3 years through energy savings.
