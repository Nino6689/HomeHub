The Problem

Worry Less, Care Better

When elderly relatives live alone, you worry. Are they eating? Did they get up okay? What if they fall? Guardian uses discreet sensors to track daily patterns and notify you if something's wrong—so you can check in when it matters, not constantly.

  • No wearables required
  • Respects privacy—no cameras needed
  • Easy for your relative to live with
Features

How Guardian Protects

Daily Routine Monitoring

Tracks when your relative wakes, eats, moves around, and settles for bed. Alerts you if patterns change—like no morning activity by 10am or no kitchen use by 1pm.

Fall Detection

Instant notification if a fall or prolonged lack of movement is detected. Faster intervention can prevent serious injury and reduce hospital stays.

Night-time Safety

Monitors essential evening tasks—lights off, doors locked, settled for the night. Get notified if bedtime routines are missed.

Customised Alerts

Every person is different. Guardian adapts to your loved one's unique routine and sends alerts tailored to their lifestyle.

Perfect For

Is Guardian Right for You?

Guardian is ideal for families who want to support an elderly relative's independence while having peace of mind. It works quietly in the background—your loved one doesn't need to do anything different.

  • Elderly parents living alone
  • Post-hospital recovery support
  • Early dementia or memory concerns
  • Families who live far away
