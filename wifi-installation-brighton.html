Frequently Asked Questions

How much does WiFi installation cost in Brighton?

WiFi installation in Brighton typically costs £300-500 for mesh WiFi setup in a flat or small home, £500-800 for larger properties or townhouses, and £800-1,500+ for full ethernet cabling installations. Period properties may require additional planning.

Can you improve WiFi in period properties?

Absolutely. Brighton's Regency and Victorian buildings often have thick walls that block WiFi signals. We use mesh WiFi systems and strategic access point placement to overcome these challenges without damaging original features.

Do you install WiFi in flats and apartments?

Yes, we install WiFi systems in Brighton flats of all sizes. We also optimise settings to reduce interference from neighbouring networks - essential in densely populated buildings.

Can you help with working from home connectivity?

Yes, reliable connectivity for video calls and remote work is essential. We ensure your home office has strong, stable WiFi or wired ethernet, with QoS to prioritise work traffic.
