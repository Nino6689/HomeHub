Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies

Last updated: February 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

How We Use Cookies

HomeHub Smart Solutions uses cookies to:

  • Remember your cookie consent preferences
  • Ensure our website functions correctly
  • Understand how visitors use our site (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you, such as setting your privacy preferences.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
hhss_cookie_consent Stores your cookie consent preferences 1 year

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

With your consent, we use Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve the site. These cookies are only set if you click "Accept All" on our cookie banner.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics - maintains session state 2 years
_clck Microsoft Clarity - user ID and preferences 1 year
_clsk Microsoft Clarity - stores session data 1 day

Third-Party Cookies

Our website uses Google Fonts to display text. Google may set cookies when loading these fonts. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of this and other websites.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Know what cookies we use and why
  • Choose whether to accept non-essential cookies
  • Change your preferences at any time
  • Have your data deleted upon request
