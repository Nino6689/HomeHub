Frequently Asked Questions

How much does smart home installation cost in Chichester?

Smart home installation in Chichester varies by scope. A starter package with voice control and smart lighting costs from £600-900. Full home automation ranges from £2,000-5,000+. We offer free consultations to design a system for your budget.

Do you work with listed buildings?

Yes, Chichester has many historic and listed buildings. We use wireless smart home technology that requires no rewiring or structural changes, preserving original features while adding modern convenience.

Which areas around Chichester do you cover?

We cover all of Chichester and surrounding areas including Bognor Regis, Selsey, the Witterings, Midhurst, Petworth, Arundel, and villages throughout the South Downs.

Can smart home technology reduce energy bills?

Yes, significantly. Smart thermostats typically save 10-15% on heating bills. Smart lighting with motion sensors eliminates waste. Many customers see payback within 2-3 years.
