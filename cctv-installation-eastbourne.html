How much does CCTV installation cost in Eastbourne?

CCTV installation in Eastbourne typically costs £600-800 for a basic 2-camera system, £900-1,400 for a 4-camera 4K system, and £1,500-2,500 for larger properties. We offer free surveys and fixed quotes with no hidden costs.

Do you install CCTV throughout Eastbourne?

Yes, we install CCTV systems throughout Eastbourne including the town centre, Old Town, Meads, Upperton, Hampden Park, Langney, and surrounding areas like Polegate, Pevensey, and Hailsham.

Are your cameras suitable for seafront properties?

Yes, we use IP67-rated weatherproof cameras that withstand coastal conditions including salt air, wind, and rain. Our cameras are ideal for Eastbourne's seafront properties and exposed locations.

Absolutely. All our systems include smartphone apps for iOS and Android. View live footage, receive instant alerts, and review recordings from anywhere - perfect for monitoring your property while away.
