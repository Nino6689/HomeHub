How much does smart home installation cost in Crawley?

Smart home installation in Crawley varies by scope. A starter package with voice control, smart lighting for 2-3 rooms, and smart plugs costs from £600-900. Full home automation with lighting, heating, security, and multi-room audio ranges from £2,000-5,000+. We offer free consultations to design systems that fit your budget.

Do you install smart homes near Gatwick Airport?

Yes, we cover all of Crawley and the Gatwick area including Three Bridges, Pound Hill, Ifield, Langley Green, Bewbush, and surrounding villages. We're based nearby in Horsham, so Crawley is one of our core service areas with no additional travel charges.

Can I control my home while travelling?

Absolutely - perfect for frequent travellers near Gatwick. Control lights, heating, and security from anywhere via smartphone. Set the heating to come on before you land, check camera feeds, and even let guests in remotely with smart locks.

Which smart home platform do you recommend?

It depends on your existing devices. Alexa offers the widest compatibility, Google excels at voice recognition, Apple HomeKit provides the best privacy and iPhone integration. We can help you choose or create hybrid systems that work across platforms.
