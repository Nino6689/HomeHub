How much does CCTV installation cost in Worthing?

CCTV installation in Worthing typically costs £600-800 for a basic 2-camera system, £900-1,400 for a 4-camera 4K system, and £1,500-2,500 for larger properties. We offer free surveys and fixed quotes with no hidden costs.

Do you install CCTV along the Worthing seafront area?

Yes, we install CCTV systems throughout Worthing including seafront properties, town centre businesses, and residential areas. We cover Goring, Ferring, Durrington, Broadwater, and all surrounding neighbourhoods.

How long does CCTV installation take?

Most residential installations are completed in a single day. A typical 4-camera system takes 4-6 hours. We schedule at your convenience and always clean up thoroughly after installation.

Can I view my Worthing home cameras from my phone?

Absolutely. All our systems include smartphone apps for iOS and Android. View live footage, receive instant alerts, and review recordings from anywhere - perfect for keeping an eye on your Worthing property when you're away.
