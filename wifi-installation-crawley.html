Frequently Asked Questions

How much does WiFi installation cost in Crawley?

WiFi installation in Crawley typically costs £300-500 for mesh WiFi setup in a medium home, £500-800 for larger homes with ethernet backhaul, and £800-1,500+ for full ethernet cabling installations. We offer free surveys to assess your specific needs and provide fixed quotes.

Can you fix WiFi dead spots in my Crawley home?

Absolutely. Dead spots are usually caused by router placement, building materials, or home layout. We survey your property, identify problem areas, and recommend the best solution - whether that's mesh WiFi, access points, or ethernet cabling.

Is mesh WiFi better than a range extender?

Yes, significantly. Range extenders halve your speed and create separate networks. Mesh WiFi maintains full speed throughout your home with seamless roaming - your devices automatically connect to the strongest signal as you move around.

Do you install ethernet cabling?

Yes, we install Cat6 and Cat6a ethernet cabling throughout Crawley homes. Wired connections provide the fastest, most reliable speeds - essential for gaming, 4K streaming, and working from home. We run cables discreetly through walls and ceilings.
